1.1 Define a Projection

For this lab we want to calculate distances between features, therefore we need a projection that preserves distance at the scale of CONUS. For this, we will use the North America Equidistant Conic:

Code 
eqdc <- '+proj=eqdc +lat_0=40 +lon_0=-96 +lat_1=20 +lat_2=60 +x_0=0 +y_0=0 +datum=NAD83 +units=m +no_defs'

Recently, Federal Agencies have claimed basic constitutional rights protected by the Fourth Amendment (protecting Americans from random and arbitrary stops and searches) do not apply fully at our borders (see Portland). For example, federal authorities do not need a warrant or suspicion of wrongdoing to justify conducting what courts have called a â€œroutine search,â€ such as searching luggage or a vehicle. Specifically, federal regulations give U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) authority to operate within 100 miles of any U.S. â€œexternal boundaryâ€. Further information can be found at this ACLU article.
